About

MY NAME IS MALCOM WRIGHT

I’m a professional concert and live event photographer based in the heart of wherever the music takes me (but my heart's in Kansas City). At 27, I’ve built my career around capturing raw, unfiltered moments—whether it’s the energy of a packed venue or the quiet intensity backstage. I live a nature-forward lifestyle that keeps me grounded and inspired, often hiking trails or chasing golden hour light with my dog, Barley, by my side. My work is fueled by a love for storytelling, connection, and the unique rhythm each artist and audience brings. From local shows to national tours, I aim to freeze those fleeting, electric moments that make live music unforgettable.
